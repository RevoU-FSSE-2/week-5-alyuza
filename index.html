Our Programs

Pre-Junior

This program is suitable for Beginner or Children aged 5 - 12 who are just starting to learn the basics of Taekwondo Martial Arts. Mental training, patience and self-defense techniques.

Join Program

Junior

This program is suitable for Intermediate or Youth aged 13 - 18 who have practiced Taekwondo before. In this program, Students will be trained to explore Taekwondo Martial Arts Techniques, and they can start participating in Regional and National Championships.

Join Program

Senior

This program is suitable for Advanced Students aged 18 and over. In this program, students will be trained to Mastering Attack and Defense Techniques. The most important thing is to learn to Control Emotions, we also have special Training for Championships.
